How to use

In order to enable our model to learn which choice of animations looks good or bad, we need to provide training examples of good choices.
Therefore, please rate each of the 5 second animations you see using the buttons above.
Before you start, please enter your name or alias to compete with others. You can check your score on the "Highscores" page.

How to interpret the labels

VERY BAD: Very bad animation that animates the wrong elements of the logo or animates them in an ugly way

BAD: Bad animation with few good approaches recognizable

OKAY: Simple but good animation, e.g. comparable to PowerPoint animations

GOOD: Cool animation but could still be improved

VERY GOOD: Perfect animation, could be used in production
